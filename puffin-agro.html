About Puffin Agro

Puffin Agro-Allied and General Services (PAAGS), founded in 2015, is a passionate agricultural company focused on delivering simple, affordable, and technology-driven solutions for food security across Africa. From input supply to full-scale crop and livestock production, we work to revolutionize agriculture at every level.

Services offered

  • Agro-Processing Solutions: We offer equipment and expertise for processing raw farm produce into finished goods. This helps reduce post-harvest losses and adds value to agricultural output.
  • Commercial Crop Cultivation: We manage and support large-scale crop farming using modern practices. Our services ensure high yield, quality output, and sustainable land use.
  • Farm Equipment & Tractor Services: We supply and lease farm machinery like tractors, planters, and harvesters. This boosts efficiency and reduces manual labor for farmers.
  • Seed & Grain Supply: PAAGS provides certified, high-yielding seeds and grains. Our products are tailored to suit local climates and enhance productivity.
  • Export & Import of Agricultural Commodities: We facilitate the movement of farm products across borders. Our team ensures quality standards for both local and international trade.
  • Poultry & Fishery Products: We offer healthy, high-quality poultry and fishery products. From chicks to fingerlings, our goal is to support protein supply chains.
  • Animal Feeds & Veterinary Supplies:  We supply nutritious animal feeds and essential medications. Our goal is to improve livestock health, weight gain, and productivity.
  • Fertilizer & Agro-Chemical Distribution: We distribute reliable fertilizers and pest control solutions. Our products support soil fertility, pest resistance, and improved crop yield.
  • Warehousing & Supply Chain Management: PAAGS provides storage and logistics for agricultural goods. We ensure timely delivery, safe storage, and reduced losses for farmers and buyers.
